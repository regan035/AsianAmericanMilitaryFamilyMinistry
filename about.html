我們的使命

美国亞裔军人家庭事工是一个以基督教信徒为基础的非盈利机构，团契的建立是為了帮助更多华裔现役，退役，退休的华裔军人和家庭。不管您来自何方，华裔军人团契尽可能地為您服務， 如果您有任何需要帮助，請不要客氣，機構里的所有義工都曾在美軍服役過，如果您有任何信仰方面的问题，欢迎咨询。我們有資深的屬靈同工會特别輔導你的問題，特别是当您的家人deploy期间，您感到壓得喘不過氣來，儘快聯絡我們，我們會和你一起為您在前綫的家人禱告，當你感到遇到不公平，甚至情緒萬般低沉時，聯絡我們，我们团契都会尽力为大家提供一個守信，信息共享，尋求真理，彼此代禱，靈修學習，一起成长的平臺，华裔军人互相帮助互相建立，愿神赐福您全家！平安！

Organization mission: The is a non-profit organization composed of Christian believers and friends. The organization has been established to help Asian American active-duty service members, reserve members, veterans, and their families. Regardless of your origin, the American Asian Military Family Ministry will help and serve you whenever you have concerns and issues about your life and beliefs. Volunteers in this organization are composed of experienced ministers, active-duties, reserves, veterans, and their family members. When you are in deployment, or feel unfairly treated, or are emotionally low, our volunteers will do our best to provide you with a platform for trust building, information sharing, truth seeking, interceding, and life developing. Our goal is to unite American Asian military members and to help ourselves.
